The play session

Experience

My first impression wasn’t particularly encouraging.

The opening cutscene struggled a little, making me wonder whether Far Cry 3 was going to be another game that pushed the RP6 too far. Thankfully, once I reached the island and gameplay began, things improved considerably.

Performance settled into a much smoother experience than I expected, making exploration and combat genuinely enjoyable. While it isn’t flawless from start to finish, the moments that matter most play surprisingly well.
