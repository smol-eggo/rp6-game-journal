Experience

If there was one game that convinced me the RP6 could deliver a proper JRPG experience, it was Ni no Kuni.

Performance was consistently smooth throughout my play sessions. Towns, exploration and battles all felt comfortable to play without constantly thinking about performance or compatibility.

The game’s beautiful art style is a perfect match for the RP6’s display. Studio Ghibli’s animation, colourful environments and soundtrack make it incredibly easy to become immersed.

More importantly…

I completely stopped thinking about the fact I was testing a game.

I was simply playing Ni no Kuni.

Exactly what this journal is about.
