Test Setup

  • Device: Retroid Pocket 6
  • Operating System: Armada
  • Store: Steam
  • Compatibility Layer: Proton

Experience

Stubbs the Zombie runs really well on the RP6, which honestly wasn’t much of a surprise. I used to play this on an old Pentium D back in the day, so I had a feeling it wouldn’t give the handheld much trouble.

What did surprise me was just how well the game holds up. The goofy zombie rampage is every bit as entertaining as I remembered, and playing it on a handheld somehow makes it even easier to get sucked into causing chaos. Controls feel great and performance never got in the way of the fun.

smol-eggo’s Verdict

Sometimes revisiting an old favourite is disappointing. Stubbs the Zombie isn’t one of those games. It still has that weird charm, it’s still a blast to play, and the RP6 feels like a perfect home for it.

Did I forget I was testing?

Yes.
