Experience

Moonlight Peaks feels like it belongs on a handheld.

For the vast majority of my time with the game, performance was smooth and it was an absolute joy to play. Exploring the village, tending to crops and soaking in the cosy atmosphere all felt perfectly suited to the RP6.

The only noticeable issue appeared during rainy weather, where performance dipped into the low 20 FPS range. Thankfully, because this is a relaxed farming game rather than a fast-paced action title, those drops were more noticeable than disruptive.

Outside of those moments, I happily kept playing.

smol-eggo’s Verdict
