The play session

Experience

Gunfire Reborn successfully launched and displayed its initial loading screen, but never progressed any further. Even after waiting several minutes, the game remained stuck and never reached the main menu.

Multiple launch attempts produced the same result, preventing any gameplay or further testing.

✦ ✦

smol-eggo’s verdict

🚧 Leave at Home

Not every compatibility test ends with a dramatic crash. Sometimes a game simply refuses to move past the starting line.

Until a future Proton update or Armada release improves compatibility, Gunfire Reborn isn’t worth installing on the RP6. At the time of testing, it wasn’t possible to get into the game at all.
