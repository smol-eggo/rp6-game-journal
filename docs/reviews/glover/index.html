Experience

My first impression wasn’t particularly positive.

The PlayStation version included with the Steam release suffered from occasional stuttering that made the experience feel rougher than I expected. Thankfully, that’s not the only version included.

Switching over to the native PC version completely changed my opinion. Performance became smooth, controls felt responsive, and suddenly I found myself enjoying the quirky platforming far more than I expected.

If you’re planning to play Glover on the RP6, do yourself a favour and launch the PC version.
