Experience

I launched CloverPit intending to see how well it ran.

Instead, I accidentally spent far longer than planned feeding coins into an increasingly questionable machine while convincing myself that the next spin would definitely be the one.

Performance remained smooth throughout my testing, allowing the game to do what it does best: trick me into believing my luck was about to change.

It never did.

But I kept playing anyway.
