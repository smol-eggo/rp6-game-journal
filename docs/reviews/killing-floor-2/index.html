Experience

Killing Floor 2 feels surprisingly at home on the RP6.

Combat is responsive, performance is enjoyable, and the game lends itself perfectly to handheld sessions. One wave quickly turns into another, and before long you’re telling yourself, “Just one more run.”

While it doesn’t completely make you forget you’re testing in the same way as a sprawling JRPG, it’s incredibly easy to lose half an hour blasting through hordes of Zeds.

The controls feel natural, the frantic action remains enjoyable, and it’s an excellent pick for shorter gaming sessions.
