The play session

Experience

Disco Elysium gave me a little bit of hope at first. It booted without any issues and I was able to make it all the way to the main menu.

Unfortunately, the moment I selected New Game, the entire Retroid Pocket 6 locked up. Not just the game, but the whole system. The device became completely unresponsive and I had to force shut it down before turning it back on.

That’s about as hard of a crash as you can get.

✦ ✦

smol-eggo’s verdict

🚧 Leave at Home

Making it to the main menu doesn’t count for much if starting a new game takes the whole handheld down with it. Until this is fixed, Disco Elysium is staying off my RP6.
