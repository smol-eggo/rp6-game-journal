Experience

If there’s one thing I can confidently say about Cuphead, it’s that the RP6 isn’t the reason you’ll lose.

Performance remained consistently smooth throughout my testing, with responsive controls and no noticeable slowdowns getting in the way. The beautifully hand-drawn animation looked fantastic on the RP6’s display, making every boss fight as gorgeous as it was frustrating.

Every defeat was entirely my own fault.
