Experience

Siralim Ultimate ran well throughout my testing, with smooth performance and no major technical issues.

The only thing that took some getting used to was the controller layout. Certain button prompts didn’t quite line up with what I expected, making the first hour feel a little awkward.

Thankfully, once I adjusted, the game became easy to enjoy, and the controls faded into the background.

smol-eggo’s Verdict

Sometimes your brain needs a software update too.

Once the controls clicked, everything else fell into place.

Did I forget I was testing?

Eventually.

The unusual button prompts only bothered me until they became muscle memory.
