Experience

I only planned to spend about thirty minutes with Date Everything! before heading to bed.

That plan quickly disappeared.

Performance was flawless throughout my time with the game, leaving me free to focus on what really mattered: forming questionable relationships with household objects. Surprisingly, playing from the RP6 actually felt more comfortable than sitting behind my PC. It suits the laid-back nature of the game perfectly and makes it incredibly easy to curl up on the sofa and lose yourself in its wonderfully bizarre world.

Sometimes the best compliment you can give a game is that the hardware completely disappears.

smol-eggo’s Verdict

There aren’t many games where dating your toaster somehow feels more natural on a handheld.

This is one of them.

Did I forget I was testing?

Yes.

I only intended to play for half an hour. The RP6 turned that into “just one more conversation.”
