Experience

Bugsnax ran beautifully throughout my testing.

Performance stayed consistently smooth, controls felt comfortable, and I never encountered anything that distracted from the experience. Instead, I spent my time exploring Snaktooth Island, meeting its wonderfully strange inhabitants, and inevitably getting the theme song stuck in my head.

The RP6 handled everything effortlessly, making this another excellent game to enjoy from the comfort of the sofa.
