Experience

Some games simply belong on a handheld.

Everhood is one of them.

Performance remained flawless throughout my testing, controls felt responsive, and before long I found myself completely immersed in its strange, colourful world. The RP6 never struggled to keep up, allowing the game’s music, rhythm and surreal atmosphere to take centre stage.

It’s one of those experiences where you stop thinking about frame rates and start wondering where the last hour went.

smol-eggo’s Verdict

The best compatibility tests are the ones that accidentally become gaming sessions.

Everhood passed that test with flying colours.

Did I forget I was testing?

Completely.

I remembered I was supposed to be benchmarking sometime after I’d already become invested in the adventure.
