Verdict

🍇 Lost Track of Time

Test Setup

  • Device: Retroid Pocket 6
  • Operating System: Armada
  • Store: Steam
  • Compatibility Layer: Proton

Experience

Some games feel like they were always meant to be portable.

Digimon Story: Cyber Sleuth is one of them.

Originally released on the PlayStation Vita, it feels completely at home on the RP6. Performance remained flawless throughout my testing, with smooth gameplay and responsive controls that made it incredibly easy to settle in for “just a few more minutes.”

Before long, I wasn’t paying attention to compatibility anymore. I was too busy building my team and getting distracted by side quests.

smol-eggo’s Verdict

If you asked me to name one JRPG that perfectly matches the RP6, Cyber Sleuth would be near the top of the list.

This feels less like making a compromise and more like finding the game’s natural home.

Did I forget I was testing?

Completely.

I only remembered after I’d already lost track of time.
