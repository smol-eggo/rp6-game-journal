The play session

Experience

Rogue Legacy 2 feels like it was made for the RP6. Runs are smooth, the game looks fantastic on the handheld, and it’s incredibly easy to fall into the “just one more run” loop.

The only oddity I ran into happens during heir selection. The upper half of the screen disappears, meaning you can’t see the class name or traits before making your choice. Unless you’ve memorized the character sprites and starting weapons, you’re essentially picking blind.

Personally, I ended up embracing it. It almost feels like an extra layer of randomness, and after a while you start recognizing classes by their appearance anyway. I can definitely see it putting some people off, but for me it became part of the charm rather than a dealbreaker.
