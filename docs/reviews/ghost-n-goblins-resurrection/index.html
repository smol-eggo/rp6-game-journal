Experience

Capcom’s RE Engine continues to impress.

Ghosts ‘n Goblins Resurrection ran flawlessly throughout my testing, maintaining smooth performance while I fought my way through the opening levels. If there’s one thing slowing the experience down, it certainly isn’t the RP6.

The game remains every bit as unforgiving as its predecessors, making every successful run feel well earned. Thankfully, performance stayed consistent enough that any mistakes were entirely my own.

smol-eggo’s Verdict

The RE Engine continues to feel like magic.

Now if only it could make me better at Ghosts ‘n Goblins.

Did I forget I was testing?

Not completely.

I kept telling myself, “One more run,” until I realised I’d been playing far longer than intended.
