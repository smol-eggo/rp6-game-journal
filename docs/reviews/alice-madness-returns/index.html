  • Store: Steam
  • Compatibility Layer: Proton

Experience

Alice: Madness Returns was a pleasant surprise on the RP6. Once I got into the game, it played smoothly and never gave me any reason to think about performance.

The only thing that stood out were the menus. They’re noticeably laggier than the gameplay itself, but since you’re only in them for a few moments at a time, it never became frustrating or got in the way of actually playing. Once I was back in Wonderland, everything just felt right.

smol-eggo’s Verdict

If someone told me the menus were running on a different device, I’d probably believe them. 😄 Thankfully, that’s where the weirdness ends. The actual game runs great, and before long I completely forgot I was supposed to be testing it.
