# Hollow Knight: Silksong

## Verdict

🍇 **Lost Track of Time**

## Test Setup

- **Device:** Retroid Pocket 6
- **Operating System:** Armada
- **Store:** Steam
- **Compatibility Layer:** Proton

---

## Experience

After all the years of waiting, the first thing I wanted to know was simple:

How does *Silksong* feel on the RP6?

The answer is wonderfully simple.

It runs beautifully.

Performance remained consistently smooth throughout my testing, allowing the gorgeous hand-drawn world and fluid combat to shine without distraction. Exploring each new area felt every bit as satisfying as I'd hoped, and before long I wasn't thinking about compatibility anymore.

I was just playing Silksong.
