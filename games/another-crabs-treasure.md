# Another Crab's Treasure

## Verdict

🛠 **Needs Tinkering**

## Test Setup

- **Device:** Retroid Pocket 6
- **Operating System:** Armada
- **Store:** Steam
- **Compatibility Layer:** Proton

---

## Experience

*Another Crab's Treasure* is technically playable, but I struggled to fully enjoy it.

Performance hovered around 30 FPS during my testing, which is certainly enough to explore the world and experience the story. However, because this is a Soulslike, responsive combat is a huge part of the experience. I found the inconsistent pacing made dodging and timing attacks feel less satisfying than they should.

If you're comfortable with lower frame rates, your experience may differ from mine. Personally, I'd rather wait until performance improves before diving in.

---

## smol-eggo's Verdict

Playable isn't always enjoyable.

For a slower RPG I'd happily accept 30 FPS, but a Soulslike asks a little more from both the player and the hardware.

No.
