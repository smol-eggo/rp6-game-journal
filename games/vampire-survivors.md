# Vampire Survivors

## Verdict

🍇 **Lost Track of Time**

## Test Setup

- **Device:** Retroid Pocket 6
- **Operating System:** Armada
- **Store:** Steam
- **Compatibility Layer:** Proton

---

## Experience

Some games feel like they were designed specifically for handhelds.

*Vampire Survivors* is one of them.

Performance was flawless throughout my testing, with no noticeable issues even as the screen filled with enemies, projectiles and enough visual effects to make the game look like controlled chaos.

The controls felt perfectly at home on the RP6, and before long I had completely forgotten I was supposed to be evaluating compatibility.

---

## smol-eggo's Verdict

If someone told me Vampire Survivors had secretly been designed around the RP6, I'd probably believe them.

It's one of those rare games where the hardware completely disappears and all that's left is "one more run."

---

## Did I forget I was testing?

Absolutely.

It felt like it was made for this thing.
